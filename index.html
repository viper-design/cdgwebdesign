Website Design

If you're like thousands of other business owners, a website is something you dread planning and setting up for your business. In fact, if you're like most people, you might say something like this:

"Making a web site is so confusing! I don't know how to program! Do I need a blog? And what the hell is 'SEO?' I don't have time to learn to make a website  AND run a business!"

Website Design and How We Help

Let's solve your problem immediately. I don't know about you, but I don't need to understand how a car works to drive it, and you don't need to understand how to write code to have a beautiful, functional website to promote your business, club or other organization. That's what we do!

Forget knowing coding.

Forget knowing CSS, HTML, or any of the other technical languages, tools, or jargon.

CDG makes it easy. We walk you through the process, holding your hand, so-to-speak. If you aren't a writer, we can do that for you, too.*

Website? or Web Site?
What's the correct way to write it? And why should you care?
Technically, it's two words. But here's why I write it as one: According to Google people search for "website" nearly twice as often as "web site".

How does our website design process work?

  • Select a website design package.
  • Choose a web design based on your chosen package.
  • Send us your logo (if you have one). If you don't have a logo, please indicate your favorite color.
  • Tell us what you have to say, or work with us to write content for your website. *
  • Send us any photos you'd like to include on your website, or choose from a large library of stock photos.
  • Give us links to any social media accounts (such as Facebook, Twitter, Google+, Linkedin, etc.) that you wish to include on your website.
  • Choose a domain name (it precedes .com) for your website - we can even help you find good options!
  • Let us create a beautiful, functional website design to grow your business!

What we do:

  • Set up hosting, domain name and guide you in purchasing email accounts.
  • Download the software and make sure it works.
  • Do it nicely (how do you like the technical jargon?)
  • Add your content and images
  • Link your social media accounts
  • Create your contact forms
  • Set up tools that make it easier for your site to be found on Google and other search engines.
  • Set up site analytics and any other additional services you order.
  • Check All
  • We can then show you how to add and change content so you don't have to pay for it in the future.

Yeah, we know, you didn't really need to read all that. You just need to know that we build great sites and handle all the details.
